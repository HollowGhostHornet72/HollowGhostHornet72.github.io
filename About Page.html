What exactly is "Get to School"

So "Get to School" is a RPG maker project created by me, Arnaldo Alicea that has been and still is in development during my time at York Tech School of Technology

Now so far for the lore and story its kind of a work and progress as the main goal of the game is to go to school. Makes sense however it cant just be that simple. Now the current direction I want it to go is a psychological route that really goes into the moral and mental state of a human on an adventure as mundaine as school. I will definitely update in case I decide to make any story changes or new thoughts about the game
